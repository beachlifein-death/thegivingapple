Donate

Your support is crucial to the success of The Giving Apple. Financial contributions help us cover operational costs, such as storage containers and transportation, and enable us to expand our reach in the community. In addition to financial support, we also welcome donations of non-perishable food items, such as canned goods and packaged snacks. Please ensure that the items are unopened and within their expiration date.

To make a financial donation, please contact us at thegivingapple@lahs.org for more information.

You can drop off your non-perishable food donations at Los Altos High School during school hours or contact us to arrange a pick-up.

We appreciate your support in our mission to reduce food waste and help those in need within our community.
