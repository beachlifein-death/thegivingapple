Donate

While The Giving Apple primarily focuses on reducing food waste by collecting untouched food from our school's lunch line, we also support local food drives in their efforts to provide meals to those in need. If you are interested in donating to The Giving Apple, please consider donating to one of our partner food drives. Your support will help them provide meals

If you would like to make a financial donation to The Giving Apple, please contact us at thegivingapple@lahs.org for more information. Your contribution will help us cover operational costs, such as storage containers and transportation, and enable us to expand our reach in the community.

In addition to financial support, we also welcome donations of non-perishable food items, such as canned goods and packaged snacks. Please ensure that the items are unopened and within their expiration date. You can drop off your donations at Los Altos High School during school hours or contact us to arrange a pick-up.

We appreciate your support in our mission to reduce food waste and help those in need within our community.
