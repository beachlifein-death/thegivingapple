The Giving Apple

Helping reduce food waste and support our community

About The Giving Apple

The Giving Apple is a student-run club at Los Altos High School, focused on reducing food waste and providing support to local food drives. By collecting untouched food from our school's lunch line, we ensure that it goes to those in need rather than being discarded.

How it Works

  1. At the end of each lunch period, club members collect untouched food from the lunch line.
  2. The collected food is properly stored and transported to our partner food drives.
  3. Our partner food drives distribute the food to those in need in our community.

Donate

If you are interested in donating to The Giving Apple, please consider donating to one of our partner food drives. Your support will help them provide meals to those in need.
